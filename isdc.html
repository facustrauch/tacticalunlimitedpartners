International Sustainable Development Corporation (ISDC)

The International Sustainable Development Corporation (“ISDC”) is mandated to activate the Living Economic System while preserving the natural environment of the Indigenous Nations by harmoniously developing Public Private Policy (“PPP”) for “sustainable development Education,

Farming and Innovation”. ISDC in charge of the Arcadia Emerald City, the custody of the pristine forest sanctuary encompassing an area of 15,000,000 Hectares and the improvement for the welfare of the Indigenous communities.

The problem

In terms of humanity

Millions of hectares of pristine lands belong to the Indigenous Nations. However, these communities continue to suffer poverty due to their lack of the needed resources and expertise necessary for the economic, incremental and sustainable development of the natural resources they hold.

In terms of environment

36 Billion Tons of CO2 are being released to the atmosphere yearly.

It is imperative to reduce emissions to counteract the devastating effects of Climate Change.

Industry adaptation means years of re-engineering, millionaire fines for the neglect of goals, and structural changes in conscience.

Carbon Credits

The Carbon Credits Bank

The CC Bank will organize, optimize and administer Carbon Credits. Carbon Credit Certificates (“CC Certs”) are coordinated and issued through the Bank acting as the highest hierarchical authority for Carbon Credits in Indigenous territories. The CC Bank will fundraise and allocate funds for impact investment in the different phases of the “Education, Farming and Innovation Master Plan” serving as the treasurer for the Natural Capital Assets and environmental steward for generations to come.

The Carbon Credit Investment Club

The Carbon Credit Investment Club serves as the organization to keep a record of the membership allowing private bidding sessions, special offers and financial products for accredited investors in Carbon Credits, Sponsors, Patrons and other institutions.

Section Carbon Credits

The Carbon Credit (”CC”) market is expected to grow 20x in the next decade, a carbon credit is a tradable permit or certificate that provides the holder of the credit the right to emit one ton of carbon dioxide or an equivalent of another greenhouse gas. ISDC is focused on utilizing CC as a sustainable currency with Humanitarian and Sustainable Development purposes and the improvement of the wellbeing of the Indigenous Nations.
