Personal Protective Equipment

As social activists, we are always thinking about the health and welfare of our planet, so we decided to create a 100% American Made anti-viral green hemp mask. The coronavirus crisis has sparked a piece of personal protective equipment (PPE) arms race around the world as governments rush to stockpile masks, gloves, visors, and gowns. Countries around the world encourage or order their citizens to wear masks in public.

For example, the popular N95 Masks are made out of synthetic polypropylene, which takes 500 years to biodegrade. The implications are a disaster for the environment and could spell years of trouble for our already environment and polluted oceans.

Over time, those products break down and add to the vast collections of microplastics in our seas, air, and food. Also, it endangers marine life ingesting it and entanglement. The irony is that, while we produce and discard plastic to fight one public health crisis, we may be slowly contributing to another. The Unlimited MED (a division of the Unlimited Hemp Company) set out to create an all-natural biodegradable hemp mask for people who care about Mother Earth. The rest is history.
