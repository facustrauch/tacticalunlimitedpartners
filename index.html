Vision

Tactical Unlimited Partner (“Tactical Unlimited”) is a game changing global firm created to address and solve through World Impact Investment global concerns.

The world is facing multiple crises which can negatively impact human civilization as we know it.

Tactical Unlimited strongly believes in the responsibility of present generations towards future generations to guarantee that 7 generations forward bequeath a planet that is not irreversibly damaged.

The shared mission within our network of entities we thrive to capitalize the power of Traditional and Natural Capital Assets by monetizing them through Asset Backed securitization ITOs, utility and rewarding tokenization systems.

To achieve our mission, Tactical Unlimited is focused on world changing projects:

- Sovereign banking and financing
- Funding multimillion dollar sustainable mining projects
- Farming developments in 2.9 million acres
- 1 trillion trees and mangroves planting programs in the next 10 years

Our network of partners entails The Earth Dollar, The Earth Bank, the Earth Sovereign Wealth Fund and the Earth Token to take action on some of the greatest concerns facing humanity, including tackling climate change, the sixth largest mass extinction event, hunger and global poverty, with the focus of fulfilling the Earth Dollar’s Enhanced Sustainable Development Goals.

The Earth Dollar is designed to rejuvenate the Earth, restore our climate, and revitalize the economy of nations. The Earth Dollar System is aligned with the 17 SDGs of the UN 2030 Sustainable Development Agenda, and has included 9 more goals to enact 26 Enhanced UN SDGs.

Tactical Unlimited Partners believes our generation is the first generation to end poverty & the last generation to save the planet. Becoming the #1 Tactical Global Accelerator focused on Sustainable Development and Impact Investment Firm.
