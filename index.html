Vision

Tactical Unlimited Partner (“Tactical Unlimited”) is a global firm created to address and solve through World Transformative Impact Investment global problems ©. The world is facing multiple crises which can negatively impact human civilization as we know it.

Tactical Unlimited strongly believes in the Responsibility of Present Generations towards future Generations to guarantee that 7 generations forward bequeath a planet that is not irreversibly damaged. The shared mission within our network of entities we thrive to capitalize the power of Traditional and Natural Capital Assets by monetizing them through Asset Backed securitization ITOs, utility and rewarding tokenization systems.

To achieve our mission, Tactical Unlimited is focused on world changing projects, i.e: 1 trillion trees and mangroves planting programs in the next 10 years, universal industrial hemp farming developments in 2.9 million acres of pristine land innovating 50,000 products to achieve “Conversion Era” objectives and substitute extractive fossil fuel production for sustainable biodegradable circular economies, and funding multimillion dollar sustainable mining projects.

In order to achieve SDGs goals, TUP is designing and executing world class projects with international first class companies and organizations. Our network of partners entails The Earth Dollar, The Earth Bank, Sovereign Wealth Fund and the Earth Token to take action on some of the greatest crisis facing humanity, including tackling climate change, the sixth largest mass extinction event, hunger and global poverty, with the focus of fulfilling the Earth Dollar’s Enhanced Sustainable Development Goals. The Earth Dollar is designed to rejuvenate the Earth, restore our climate, revitalize the economy of nations.
